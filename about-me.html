About Me

Hi! I’m Levona and I’m a senior student (graduating December 2018) studying Interactive Arts & Technology (IAT) at SFU with a two year chemistry background. My passion lies in visual design and product design. My experience include doing design internships at the Mothers Matter Centre, the City of Surrey, and Scentuals Body Care.

I love to explore and experiment by doing hands-on work. I believe design is a way of thinking as well as communicating an idea to a broader audience. I'm passionate in working on real world problems from complex social issues to improving experiences and services that people encounter in their daily lives. I believe design encompasses every aspect of our society and we can continue to improve it.

On the side, I also create custom cards for peers and coworkers. I also enjoy playing badminton on a weekly basis and exploring new food places to try.
